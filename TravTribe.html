Imagine never traveling alone again. TravTribe connects solo travelers with like-minded companions who share your interests, travel style, and budget. It takes the guesswork out of finding the perfect travel buddy, making your adventures more enjoyable and memorable.

User Research

This research project involved planning and conducting five interviews with single travelers to gather data. Participants were interviewed either via Zoom or in person. Demographic information was collected using Google Forms. The data obtained was used to develop a user persona.

Guerilla testing

GoalsEvaluate TravTribe's app for user-friendliness and intuitive design. 

Tasks: 1. Create a user account 

2. Find the chat 

3. Find edit profile option 

4. Log out 

Observation- Users created their accounts successfully. - Users easily found the chat icon. - Users struggled to locate the edit profile option. - Users had trouble finding the log out button.ons

Takeaways

This project has been an incredible learning experience. Through conducting interviews, I discovered the importance of asking detailed follow-up questions and the value of thorough preparation. These lessons will shape my approach for future projects.
