TravTribe

App Design

Imagine never traveling alone again. TravTribe connects solo travelers with like-minded companions who share your interests, travel style, and budget. It takes the guesswork out of finding the perfect travel buddy, making your adventures more enjoyable and memorable.

SocialQuest

App Design

Planning an adventure should be exciting, not exhausting. SocialQuest is your go-to platform for discovering nearby, fun experiences with a like-minded community. With curated activities and the best deals at your fingertips, you’ll spend less time researching and more time enjoying the journey.

U.S. Department of Education

Website Design

Navigating student loans and grants shouldn’t be stressful. This project redesigns the Department of Education website to make financial information accessible for parents of high school seniors, helping families make smarter financial decisions with ease.
